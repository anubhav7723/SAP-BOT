TOPIC: SAP MM - Vendor Master Data
MODULE: Materials Management (MM) / Financial Accounting (FI) overlap
SCOPE: ECC 6.0 and S/4HANA

OVERVIEW
The Vendor Master holds all data needed to conduct business with a supplier -
address, purchasing terms, bank details, tax information, and accounting
(reconciliation account) data. Like the material master, it is structured in
levels: General Data (client level), Company Code Data (accounting), and
Purchasing Organization Data.

ORGANIZATIONAL LEVELS
- General Data: name, address, control data, bank details - valid across the
  whole client. Table: LFA1.
- Company Code Data: reconciliation account, payment terms, dunning data.
  Table: LFB1.
- Purchasing Organization Data: order currency, incoterms, purchasing group
  default, vendor-specific purchasing data. Table: LFM1.
- Bank details: LFBK.

S/4HANA CHANGE - BUSINESS PARTNER (BP)
A major architectural change in S/4HANA is that Vendor Master maintenance
(T-code XK01/XK02/XK03 and MK01/MK02/MK03) is being replaced by the
Business Partner model (T-code BP). In S/4HANA, vendors (and customers) are
technically created/maintained as Business Partners with a "Vendor" (or
"Supplier") role, and the system synchronizes data into the classic LFA1/
LFB1/LFM1 tables in the background via a integration layer (this sync is
sometimes referred to as the Customer/Vendor Integration, CVI).
In newer S/4HANA releases, the classic vendor T-codes (XK01 etc.) are
blocked/hidden by default and BP is the only supported entry point. Exact
behavior (whether XK01 still works) depends on release and configuration -
verify in the specific target system.

KEY TABLES
- LFA1: Vendor master (general data) - LIFNR (vendor number), NAME1, LAND1, SPRAS.
- LFB1: Vendor master (company code data) - LIFNR, BUKRS, AKONT (reconciliation account), ZTERM (payment terms).
- LFM1: Vendor master (purchasing org data) - LIFNR, EKORG, WAERS (currency).
- LFBK: Vendor bank details - LIFNR, BANKS, BANKL, BANKN.
- WYT3: Vendor sub-range (rarely used).
- BUT000 / BUT020 (etc.): Business Partner general data tables (S/4HANA BP model).

KEY FIELDS
- LIFNR: Vendor account number (typically CHAR 10 in ECC; extended in S/4HANA
  similarly to MATNR in some configurations - verify per system).
- EKORG: Purchasing Organization.
- AKONT: Reconciliation account (G/L account that vendor postings sum into).

RELATED CONCEPTS
- Vendor Account Group: controls number ranges and which fields/screens are
  relevant for a vendor (e.g., one-time vendor vs. regular vendor).
- Partner Functions for vendors (e.g., Ordering Address, Goods Supplier,
  Invoicing Party, Alternative Payee) - relevant in purchasing document partner
  determination, conceptually parallel to SD customer partner functions.
- Source List / Info Record link a vendor to a material for procurement
  (see MM_Purchasing_Process.txt).

TOPIC: SAP MM - Material Master Data
MODULE: Materials Management (MM)
SCOPE: ECC 6.0 and S/4HANA (differences noted where relevant)

OVERVIEW
The Material Master is the central repository of information a company maintains
about every material it procures, produces, stores, sells, or uses. It is
organized into "views," each maintained by a different business function
(Purchasing, MRP, Accounting, Sales, Storage, etc.), and each view can be
maintained at a different organizational level (client, plant, storage location,
sales org, valuation area).

ORGANIZATIONAL LEVELS OF MATERIAL DATA
- Client level: data valid across the whole system (e.g., base unit of measure,
  material description, material group).
- Plant level: data specific to a plant (e.g., MRP type, procurement type,
  lot size, purchasing group).
- Storage location level: stock quantities per storage location.
- Sales organization / distribution channel level: sales-specific data
  (delivering plant, sales unit, tax classification).
- Valuation area level (usually = plant): valuation class, price control,
  standard/moving average price.

KEY MATERIAL MASTER VIEWS
- Basic Data: material description, base unit of measure, material group,
  gross/net weight, division.
- Purchasing: purchasing group, order unit, GR processing time.
- MRP 1-4: MRP type, reorder point, lot size, safety stock, planned delivery time.
- Sales: Sales Org 1/2, Sales: General/Plant view - delivering plant, item
  category group, availability check group.
- Accounting 1/2: valuation class, price control (S = standard, V = moving avg),
  standard price, moving average price.
- Storage: storage conditions, batch management indicator.
- Warehouse Management: storage type, picking area (if WM/EWM active).
- Quality Management: QM procurement active flag, inspection setup.
- Work Scheduling: production-related data (used more on PP side).

MATERIAL TYPES (examples)
- ROH: Raw material
- HALB: Semi-finished product
- FERT: Finished product
- HAWA: Trading goods
- VERP: Packaging material
- DIEN: Service
- ERSA: Spare part
Material type controls which views are relevant, number range, and
default settings (e.g., quantity/value updating).

KEY TABLES (see also MM_Key_Tables.txt for full field-level detail)
- MARA: General material data (client level) - MATNR, MTART, MATKL, MEINS, BRGEW, NTGEW.
- MAKT: Material descriptions (language-dependent) - MATNR, SPRAS, MAKTX.
- MARC: Plant data for material - MATNR, WERKS, DISMM (MRP type), BESKZ (procurement type).
- MARD: Storage location data / stock - MATNR, WERKS, LGORT, LABST (unrestricted stock).
- MBEW: Material valuation - MATNR, BWKEY, VPRSV (price control), STPRS (standard price), VERPR (moving avg price).
- MVKE: Sales data for material - MATNR, VKORG, VTWEG, VERSG (statistics group).
- MEAN: International article numbers (EANs/UPCs) linked to material.
- MLGN / MLGT: Warehouse management material data (if classic WM active).

MATNR FIELD LENGTH NOTE (S/4HANA)
In classic ECC, MATNR (material number) is CHAR 18. Starting with S/4HANA,
SAP extended the material number field length to CHAR 40 (the "Material
Number Field Length Extension," SAP Note-driven, optional/activatable feature
depending on release). Not all S/4HANA systems have this extended by default -
this is a configurable/activatable extension, so always verify the actual
field length in the target system's SE11 rather than assuming.

RELATED CONCEPTS
- Material Group (MATKL): higher-level classification used for reporting and
  purchasing analytics.
- Batch Management: if activated, stock is tracked at batch level (table MCHB,
  batch master MCH1/MCHA).
- Cross-Plant Material Status / Plant-Specific Material Status: controls
  whether a material can be procured, sold, etc.
